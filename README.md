# Inventory & Sales Performance Analysis

## 📌 Project Overview
A comprehensive data analysis project focused on evaluating sales performance, inventory profitability, and product-level metrics. Designed with a sleek, modern dark interface, this dashboard provides an intuitive, high-level overview of revenue trends and deep-dives into sub-category performance to identify growth drivers and profit bottlenecks.

## 📊 Core KPIs

1- * **Total Sales:** 2M

2- * **Total Profit:** 286K

3- * **Overall Profit Margin:** 12.5%

4- * **Total Orders:** 5,009

5- * **Total Units Sold:** 38K

6- * **Average Order Value (AOV):** 8

7- * **Total Products:** 1,818

8- * **Losing Products:** 296

## 💡 Key Business Insights

1- * **Historical Sales Trends:** Sales demonstrate a consistent upward trajectory from January 2015 through late 2018, reaching a notable peak of 118.4K.

2- * **Regional Dominance:** The West region is the most profitable market, generating 108K in total profit, followed by the East at 92K. Conversely, the Central region underperforms with only 40K in profit.

3- * **Top Performing Categories:** "Technology" is the leading category, contributing the highest share of total sales at 36.4% (836.15K). It is also the most profitable overall category, generating 145.45K.

4- * **Sub-Category & Product Leaders:** "Copiers" lead the sub-category profitability with 56K. At the product level, the "Canon imageCLASS 2200 Advanced Copier" stands out as the absolute top-performing product.

5- * **Profit Drainers:** Out of 1,818 products, 296 are currently operating at a loss. The "Tables" sub-category is the biggest drain on revenue, generating a negative profit of -18K, followed by Bookcases (-3K) and Supplies (-1K).


## 🚀 Strategic Recommendations
1- **Address Losing Inventory:** Immediately investigate the 296 losing products. A strategic decision must be made regarding the "Tables" sub-category (-18K loss); options include increasing prices, reducing supplier costs, or discontinuing the line entirely to protect the overall profit margin.
2- **Capitalize on Technology & Copiers:** Given that Technology and Copiers are driving the bulk of the profit, marketing budgets and inventory space should be heavily skewed toward these high-performing segments.
3- **Regional Optimization:** Conduct a localized market analysis for the Central and South regions. Formulate targeted promotional campaigns to elevate their profitability closer to the benchmarks set by the West and East regions.
